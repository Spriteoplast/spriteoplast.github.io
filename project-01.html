Horizontal shearing lines are generated in the ground and projected to the stereotomic form, then sheared vertically, revealed by the orange and yellow colors. Generative shearing in the ground and stereotomic form create the complexity of the model. Plan, elevation, and section views display the results of successively shearing. Focusing in on the micro-scale of the design reveals how people can navigate through the space, and how there are both oppressive and free spaces within the model.
